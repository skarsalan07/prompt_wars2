# Demo Script and 3-Minute Pitch

## 3-Minute Demo Script
1. Open the dashboard in `Judge Demo` mode.
2. Show that the product immediately surfaces a top hidden trigger, current burnout risk, forecast horizon, and motivation trend.
3. Switch between JEE, NEET, and UPSC personas to prove exam-specific coaching and different emotional narratives.
4. Open Analytics and point to the trigger clustering chart, emotional heatmap, and weekly pattern discovery cards.
5. Open Recovery and show that the plan is automatically tailored to trigger focus and exam proximity.
6. Open Coach and generate a personalized response grounded in the student’s stress history.
7. Close by explaining that anonymous-first mode lets real students start privately, while authenticated mode supports durable persistence and merge flows.

## 3-Minute Pitch
This solution wins because it does more than log how a student feels. It reveals why emotional strain is happening, predicts when burnout is coming, and responds with exam-specific support that feels personal instead of generic.

Traditional mood trackers capture isolated emotions. Our platform builds a Mental Wellness Intelligence Layer on top of journaling, mood logs, sleep, study consistency, and stress-related reflections. It clusters hidden triggers like family pressure, fear of failure, poor mock-test performance, and lack of sleep. It then forecasts burnout risk over the next 7 and 30 days, surfaces emotional patterns over time, and generates tailored recovery plans for JEE, NEET, UPSC, GATE, CAT, and CUET aspirants.

We optimized the implementation for the actual judging rubric: strong problem alignment, modular TypeScript code, deterministic analytics for explainability, secure prompt handling, high test coverage on scoring-critical logic, and an accessible interface. Most importantly, seeded judge demo personas make the insight quality obvious within seconds, which means judges do not need to imagine the product’s value. They can see it immediately.
